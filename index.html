    Weekly Diet Plan

    • Monday: Oats + eggs, grilled chicken salad, rice with fish and vegetables
    • Tuesday: Greek yogurt + nuts, quinoa bowl, paneer with whole wheat roti
    • Wednesday: Smoothie + toast, turkey sandwich, lentils with brown rice
    • Thursday: Omelet + fruit, chickpea salad, grilled tofu and veggies
    • Friday: Oat pancakes, chicken wrap, salmon with sweet potato
    • Saturday: Poha + peanuts, mixed bean bowl, lean beef stir-fry
    • Sunday: Recovery meal day with balanced protein, carbs, and hydration

    Protein-Rich Foods

    • Eggs, chicken breast, fish, lean beef
    • Greek yogurt, paneer, cottage cheese
    • Lentils, chickpeas, kidney beans, soy chunks
    • Tofu, tempeh, nuts, seeds, peanut butter
    • Whey protein (if needed for convenience)

    Nutrition Tips for Fitness

    • Eat 1.6 to 2.2g protein per kg body weight for muscle support.
    • Prioritize whole foods and limit processed sugar.
    • Hydrate throughout the day, especially before workouts.
    • Take a pre-workout meal 60-90 minutes before training.
    • Post-workout meal should include protein + carbs for recovery.
    • Track progress weekly and adjust calories gradually.
